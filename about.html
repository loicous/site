About

LOICOUS, a French-born producer from Lille, began making music in 2018. He blends R&B and neo-soul samples with bouncy drums, drawing inspiration from artists like Budgie, Devin Morrison, KAYTRANADA, and Sam Gellaitry. In March 2024, he released his debut EP, SOUL FOOD, and has produced tracks for various R&B and hip-hop artists, including Ceddy,Rizzy Wallace, and Ta-Ra.
